AutoVaxx

A multi-platform mobile application that automates the booking process for Covid-19 vaccinations at privatized Canadian pharmacies such as Shoppers Drug Mart and Rexall. Utilizes a web crawler and scraper to fetch live appointment data from these pharmacies then relays that information to a NodeJS server which stores the data in a Firestore DB. Users essentially enter their data once and can book as many appointments as they want. It was created during the height of the pandemic when bookings were impossible to get due to demand and each individual waitlist required you to fill out the form through their portal. Allowed users to waitlist at numerous pharmacies within seconds.
